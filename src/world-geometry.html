# World geometry We need coordinates! Let's agree that the unit will be the width of our hero: * When the hero goes North or South he changes it's __Z__ coordinate. * When the hero goes East or West he changes it's __X__ coordinate. * When the hero goes Up or Down he changes it's __Y__ coordinate.
